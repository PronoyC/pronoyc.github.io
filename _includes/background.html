A bit about me...

I'm currently a computing and financial management undergraduate at the University of Waterloo (also pursuing a minor in combinatorics and optimization), learning how to create powerful code and useful technical products. I have so far completed three internships at Wawanesa Insurance, Deloitte, and LCBO|next.

As a full-stack developer, I enjoy bridging powerful back-end code with beautiful front-end design — combining my technical knowledge with my keen eye for design to create a beautiful product. My goal is to always build applications that are scalable and efficient under the hood while providing engaging, pixel-perfect user experiences. In addition, I love using blockchain technologies whenever possible, including Corda, Fabric, and Ethereum.

As a product manager, I have an obsessive desire to design and ship consumer-loving and money-making apps. As any cultured person, I use Agile for the best product development process, consistently acting as a liasion between strategy, design, and development teams while juggling product roadmaps and a venti vanilla ice coffee. I pride myself in being a collaborator — not a dictator, supporting my team and helping them grow while maintaining tight deadlines. Being from a technical background, I am fluent in understanding tech-speak and through my extensive UX experiences, I live and breathe consumer journey maps. I always look for the simplest, cost-effective solution — even if it isn't technical.

When I'm not developing or ideating, I'm probably writing a Medium article, helping the Stack Overflow community, browsing Reddit, watching NBA games, or racking up some wins in Overwatch.
