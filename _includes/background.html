A bit about me...

I'm currently a computing and financial management undergraduate at the University of Waterloo (also pursuing a double minor in computational mathematics and combinatorics & optimization), learning how to create useful software products. I have so far completed six internships at Wawanesa Insurance, Deloitte, LCBO, Bidvine, and Purolator.

As a product manager, I have an obsessive desire to design and ship amazing products. As any cultured person, I use Agile for a smooth product development process, consistently acting as a liasion between business, design, and development teams while juggling product roadmap drafts and a venti vanilla ice coffee. I pride myself in being a collaborator — not a dictator, supporting my team and helping them grow while maintaining tight deadlines. Being from a technical background, I am fluent in understanding tech-speak and through my extensive UX experiences, I live and breathe user personas and journey maps. I always look for the simplest, cost-effective solution that addresses user needs — even if it isn't technical.

When I'm not developing or ideating, I'm probably writing an article on Medium, helping the Stack Overflow community, browsing Reddit, watching NBA games, or racking up some wins in Overwatch.
